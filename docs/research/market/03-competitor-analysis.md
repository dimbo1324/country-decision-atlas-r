SECTION 4. THE HEADLINE TAKEAWAY ON THE COMPETITIVE FIELD
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

  NO SINGLE PLATFORM combines, all at once:
    1. Objective country indicators
    2. Visa/residency/citizenship pathways
    3. Tracking legal and political change
    4. Structured human relocation experience
    5. Personalized scenario matching
    6. Explainable scoring/ranking
    7. A country's trend over time (Country Drift)
    8. Evidence-backed source links
    9. Alerts and watchlists

  Existing strong solutions are fragmented by segment. The market
  gap is obvious: a hybrid platform that treats choosing a country
  as a decision-support workflow, not a static article, a forum,
  a cost-of-living table, or a visa-service funnel.

  Remote (EOR)              $599/employee/mo.

  Cartus, Graebel, Sirva     Enterprise concierge

  CONSULTING:
  Henley & Partners          Large capital + advisory fees
  Global Citizen Solutions   On request
  Immigration lawyers        $150–$500+/hr (jurisdiction-dependent)

  TAKEAWAY FOR CDA:
  • B2C freemium + a premium subscription: $9–49/mo. or $49–199/yr
  • Paid reports: $19–99 for a personalized PDF/country
  • A B2B API: $300–1,000+/mo., following Numbeo's model
  • A marketplace: a 10–20% transaction fee
