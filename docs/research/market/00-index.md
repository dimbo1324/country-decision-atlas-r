KEY FIGURES (SUMMARY)
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

  MARKET SIZE:
  • 304 million international migrants (2024, UN DESA)
  • +55–60% growth over the last 20 years
  • ~2.1% average annual growth
  • Migrants' share of world population: 3.7%

  TARGET SEGMENT (DIGITAL NOMADS):
  • 18.5 million American digital nomads (2025, MBO Partners)
  • +153% growth from 2019 to 2025
  • Dozens of countries have launched dedicated visas

  STUDENT MOBILITY:
  • ~7.3 million students studying abroad
  • Tripled over 20 years (UNESCO)
  • The UK, Canada, and Australia are tightening requirements

  DESIRE TO MIGRATE:
  • 33% of adults in Sub-Saharan Africa (Gallup 2025)
  • 25% in Latin America and the Caribbean (Gallup 2025)
  • The US remains the #1 desired country, but at only 15% (a 20-year low)

  FORCED DISPLACEMENT:
  • 123.2 million forcibly displaced people (UNHCR, end of 2024)

  MARKET PRICE BENCHMARKS:
  • Numbeo API Basic: $260/mo.
  • Numbeo API Pro: $480/mo.
  • Nomads.com: ~$99/year
  • International Living: ~$49–129/year
  • Remote (EOR): $599/employee/mo.

════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
KEY TAKEAWAYS FOR THE PRODUCT
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

  1. The migration market is information-saturated but poorly
     integrated. The data exists — a decision system doesn't.

  2. No existing product combines, all at once:
     objective data, legal changes, country direction,
     a personalized scenario, and an explainable decision output.

  3. Users' most painful points:
     — stale information
     — contradictory advice
     — no clear picture of the path to permanent residency/citizenship
     — not understanding the tax consequences

  4. People don't pay for information — they pay for reducing the
     risk of an irreversible decision.

  5. The B2B potential is significant: HR teams, law firms,
     fintech, insurance, relocation agencies.
