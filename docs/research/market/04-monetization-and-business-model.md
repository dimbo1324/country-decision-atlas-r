SECTION 1. THE INDUSTRY'S MONETIZATION STRUCTURE
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

  The migration-platform market has three monetization layers:

  LAYER 1 — INFORMATION (Consumer):
  Subscriptions, reports, guides, premium filters, community access.

  LAYER 2 — TRANSACTIONAL (Services):
  Legal consultations, visa services, tax advice,
  insurance, banking, logistics, housing, schools, agents.

  LAYER 3 — B2B / DATA:
  HR mobility SaaS, legal-analytics subscriptions,
  an API, corporate relocation programs, data licensing.

SECTION 2. FREE VS. PAID — THE MARKET PATTERN
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

  TYPICALLY FREE:
  • Basic country/city articles
  • Visa overview lists
  • Public forums (viewing)
  • Cost-of-living rankings
  • Some lead-magnet reports
  • Official (government) legal resources
  • Basic calculators

  TYPICALLY PAID:
  • API / data access
  • Bulk exports
  • Detailed reports
  • Premium membership
  • Legal/tax consultations
  • Visa filing
  • Residency/citizenship advisory
  • Managing corporate cases
  • EOR / payroll / compliance
  • Policy alerts and tracking
  • Enterprise dashboards

SECTION 4. THE RECOMMENDED MONETIZATION MODEL FOR CDA
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

  PRINCIPLE: Don't hide baseline facts behind a paywall.
  Monetize reduced uncertainty, time saved, data freshness, and
  routing to verified specialists.

  ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  TIER 1 — FREE
  ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  • Country Cards (basic)
  • Country comparison (limited)
  • Links to public sources
  • Limited user stories
  • A basic cost-of-living and visa overview
  • Weekly public legal updates

  Goal: acquire users, demonstrate data quality.

  ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  TIER 2 — PREMIUM (Consumer)
  ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  • A personalized country shortlist
  • The full Scenario Simulator
  • Deeper modeling (visa/tax/cost by persona)
  • A watchlist and alerts
  • A document checklist
  • An AI summary with sources
  • Community-story filters
  • A relocation timeline

  Price benchmark: $9–29/mo. or $49–149/yr

  ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  TIER 3 — PAID REPORTS
  ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  Persona-specific country reports:
  • A digital nomad in Uruguay
  • A family in Germany
  • An entrepreneur in the UAE
  • An investor in Portugal
  • A retiree in Spain

  + "Country Direction" reports
  + Visa-route reports
  + Relocation budgets
  + Legal-change reports

  Price benchmark: $19–99 per report

  ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  TIER 4 — MARKETPLACE
  ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  A transaction fee on:
  • Lawyer and tax-advisor consultations
  • Insurance
  • Banking / fintech
  • Logistics
  • Housing search
  • Document translation and apostilles
  • Language courses
  • Company registration

  Fee benchmark: 10–20% of the transaction

  ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  TIER 5 — B2B (Business)
  ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  • A Country Intelligence API
  • A legal-change feed
  • A Relocation Risk Dashboard
  • An employee country-choice assistant
  • An HR Global Mobility Watchlist
  • Anonymized demand insights
  • White-label country cards

  Benchmark: $300–2,000+/mo. (scale-dependent)

SECTION 5. HIGH-VALUE PAID FEATURES
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

  Paid features with the highest perceived user value:

  ★★★★★  A personal eligibility check
  ★★★★★  Change alerts (visa, tax, law)
  ★★★★★  A path to permanent residency/citizenship with a timeline
  ★★★★☆  A tax and employer-risk pre-screen
  ★★★★☆  A real 90-day budget covering every expense
  ★★★★☆  A marketplace of verified specialists
  ★★★★☆  A country watchlist with alerts
  ★★★☆☆  An AI summary with source grounding
  ★★★☆☆  B2B dashboards and an API
  ★★★☆☆  A deadline tracker (renewals, registration, taxes)

════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
SECTION 6. WEAK PAID FEATURES (WHAT TO AVOID)
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

  ✗  Generic "top-10 countries" lists with no personalization
  ✗  Stale PDF guides with no updates
  ✗  Paid access to city rankings with no transparent methodology
  ✗  Community access with no active moderation
  ✗  Visa summaries that just copy official pages
  ✗  AI answers with no sources
  ✗  Recommendations with undisclosed affiliate incentives
  ✗  Generic relocation courses with no personalization
