Notes on source reliability
- Highest confidence: UN DESA, OECD, ILO, UNESCO, UNHCR, World Bank, national government portals, and official legal texts.
- Medium confidence: Gallup, Pew, IOM/Migration Data Portal, InterNations, HSBC, academic papers, recognised media such as Reuters.
- Directional only: market research firms, SEO articles, relocation-company indices, community posts, influencer content.
- Use Google Trends as relative interest, not absolute demand.
- Use Reddit/Telegram/Facebook/YouTube as qualitative behaviour signals, not precise population statistics unless platform analytics are available.
