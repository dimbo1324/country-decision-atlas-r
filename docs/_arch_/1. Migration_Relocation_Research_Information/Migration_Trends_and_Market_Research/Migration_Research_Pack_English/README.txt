Important limitations:
- Google Trends and social media community counts require direct export/API measurement for exact time-series data.
- Some commercial market-size figures are directional estimates from market-research providers, not audited public statistics.
- Country-specific legal information changes frequently; all legal content should be rechecked before product launch or user-facing advice.
