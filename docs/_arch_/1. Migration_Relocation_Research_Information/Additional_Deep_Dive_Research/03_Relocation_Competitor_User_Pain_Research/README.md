# Relocation User Pain, Competitor and Monetisation Research Package

Prepared: 2026-06-17  
Language: English  
Format: Markdown and TXT files inside a ZIP archive

This package answers the research questions in blocks **5–8**:

- **5. User pains** — what information is scattered, where people get outdated or contradictory advice, what mistakes they make, and what tools reduce relocation risk.
- **6. Direct and adjacent competitors** — country-selection, cost-of-living, quality-of-life, visa, digital nomad, expat community, legal-tracking, corporate relocation and data/report platforms.
- **7. Deep platform analysis** — what each major platform solves, who uses it, what features are strong/weak, and whether it has comparison, personalisation, AI, community, evidence, legal tracking, alerts and dashboards.
- **8. Competitor monetisation** — free vs paid features, subscriptions, reports, consultations, marketplaces, affiliate/B2B/API models, pricing signals and perceived value.

## Files

1. `00_Methodology_and_Limitations.md` — evidence rules, limitations and verification logic.
2. `01_User_Pains_Q5.md` — detailed answers to all user-pain questions.
3. `02_Competitor_Landscape_Q6.md` — direct and adjacent competitor map.
4. `03_Platform_Deep_Dives_Q7.md` — deep-dive profiles and feature matrix.
5. `04_Monetisation_Q8.md` — pricing, business models and paid-value analysis.
6. `05_Product_Opportunity_Map.md` — product implications for a comprehensive country-choice platform.
7. `Source_Index.md` — source list with URLs.
8. `ALL_ANSWERS_COMBINED.md` — all files combined for easy reading.

TXT copies are included for easier copying into other tools.

## Important caveat

This is not legal, tax, immigration, investment or financial advice. Immigration rules, tax regimes, consular practice, housing markets and platform prices change frequently. Treat this as a strategic research brief and verify country-specific decisions against official government sources and qualified professionals before acting.
