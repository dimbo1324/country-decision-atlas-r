## Important limitation

This package is strategic product research. It is not legal, tax, investment, immigration, medical, or financial advice. The output proposes what a relocation decision-support platform should collect, verify, compare, and explain. Before turning any legal or visa information into user-facing recommendations, each item must be checked against official sources and, where needed, reviewed by a qualified lawyer or tax adviser.
