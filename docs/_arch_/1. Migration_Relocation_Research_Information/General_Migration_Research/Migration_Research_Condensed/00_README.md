## Key Numbers at a Glance

- **304 million** international migrants worldwide (2024, UN)
- **123.2 million** forcibly displaced globally (record, end 2024, UNHCR)
- **~900 million** adults globally expressed desire to emigrate permanently (Gallup)
- **21%** of Americans want to move abroad (2024 record high, Gallup)
- **45+** countries now offer dedicated digital nomad visa programs (2025)
- **$17.67 billion** — size of the Employee Relocation Services market (2024)
- **2.6 million** highly skilled professionals crossed borders in 2024 (BCG)
- **6.2 million** new permanent immigrants to OECD countries (2024, historically high)
- **$831 billion** in international remittances (2022, up 650% from 2000)
- **40%** of expats return home within 3 years (social isolation primary factor)

## Usage Notes

- Immigration rules change **rapidly** — always verify visa/residency specifics against official government sources before acting on this information.
- Tax information is general guidance only; always consult a qualified tax professional for personal situations.
- Cost-of-living figures reflect 2024–2025 data; prices in popular expat destinations have risen significantly since 2020–2021.
- This research is organized for **product research, market analysis, and content planning** purposes.
