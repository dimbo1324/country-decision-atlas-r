## Source hierarchy used in the pack

1. **Highest authority for quantitative migration data:** UN DESA, IOM, UNHCR, OECD, World Bank.
2. **Highest authority for laws and visas:** national government immigration portals, official gazettes, parliamentary/legal briefings.
3. **Strong market sources:** Mercer, Fragomen, KPMG, PwC/EY-style mobility updates, Localyze, Jobbatical.
4. **Useful but biased community sources:** Reddit, YouTube, Telegram, Facebook, expat blogs.
5. **Use with caution:** SEO visa guides, influencer videos, agency articles without update dates, old forum threads.
